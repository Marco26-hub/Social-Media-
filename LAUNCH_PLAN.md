## 🚨 RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: System doesn't work in production
- **Mitigation:** Heavy testing in Soft Launch, 2-3 week buffer
- **Plan B:** Roll back to manual systems if needed

### Risk 2: Customers don't see ROI in 30 days
- **Mitigation:** Set realistic expectations, show case studies, daily check-ins
- **Plan B:** Offer full refund if no leads generated

### Risk 3: Competitor copies feature
- **Mitigation:** Unlikely in 6 months (complex system), but get to market FAST
- **Plan B:** Innovation moat → keep building more agents

### Risk 4: Not enough inbound demand
- **Mitigation:** Paid ads from week 6, affiliate partners, referral program
- **Plan B:** Hire sales person, do cold outreach
